Target Actual Control Market TACM

Target Actual Control Market (TACM) is a value added service for the Producers of Fruit and Vegetables. TACM enforces better predictability in terms of the final price of the product as well as improved control in terms of the cycle times of the end to end process for the products.

The Management Information produced from our result orientated user friendly system will enable Producers and Agents to serve the wider society more effectively.
